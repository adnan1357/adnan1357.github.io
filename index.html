Bootcamp 2020: Forest scene

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of arrays, loops, and randomness. I created a small game where you are a small ball and you have to collect as much food as you can while avoiding the bouncing balls. The bouncing balls have a random trajectory and the food respawn in random locations. The score is counted and saved.
